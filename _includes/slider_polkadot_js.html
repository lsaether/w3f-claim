Directly From polkadot.js

WARNING: This is the least secure method of generating an address. It should only be used if there are no other options available, and you are aware of the risks involved, up to and including total loss of any DOTs or KSM stored in the account.

It is possible to generate an address directly from the polkadot.js app, without any additional security measures such as that provided by having an air-gapped device or a separate plugin.

Step 1

Go To polkadot.js > Accounts

On your computer browser, navigate to https://polkadot.js.org/apps and click Accounts on the sidebar.

Step 2 Multiple

Start Generation of the Account

Click on the “Add Account” button

Step 3

Store the Key Somewhere Safe

This may sound repetitive, but it is important to realize that your seed is the key to your entire account. Someone who knows your seed has uncontested control over your account.

On the other hand, if you lose your seed, and your mobile device fails, you will have no way of accessing your account. The Web3 Foundation will not be able to override this - any DOTs in that account will not be accessible to you until you find the seed.

Thus, it is imperative to store the seed somewhere safe, secret, and secure. If your device fails for some reason, you can simply re-enter your seed on a new device after following the setup instructions here, and have access to your account once again.

For maximum security, the seed should be written down on paper or another non-digital device and stored in a safe place. You may also want to protect your seed from physical damage, as well (e.g. by storing in a sealed plastic bag to prevent water damage, storing it in a fireproof safe, etc.) It is recommended that you store multiple copies of the seed in geographically separate locations (e.g., one in your home safe and one in a safety deposit box at your bank).

You should definitely not store your seed on any kind of computer which has or may have access to the Internet in the future.

Step 4 Multiple

Create and Backup Account

Click “Save” and your account will be created. It will also generate a backup file which you should save to your computer. Ideally, you would also save it on an external hard drive or thumb drive, or print it out and be able to re-enter it later. You should not store it in cloud storage, email it to yourself, etc.

You can use this backup file to restore your account. This backup file is not readable except by decrypting it with the password.
