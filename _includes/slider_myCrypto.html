MyCrypto

These instructions will help you send a transaction from MyCrypto. MyCrypto is a flexible and secure Ethereum wallet that allows you to send a transaction from your Ethereum account and supports many different key storage formats.

Install MyCrypto

It is strongly recommended to download and use the MyCrypto application locally. For some types of key storage formats this is the only option. Downloading the application locally allows you to perform the signing steps offline if preferred.

Ensure that you download the official program from MyCrpyto official sources. You can always find the most up-to-date releases of the desktop app on their releases page. Choose the one that matches your operating system architecture.

Interacting with Contracts

Once the MyCrypto application is installed, open it and navigate to the Contracts tab. If you're using the locally downloaded app this will appear as a sidebar on your application screen. If you're using the browser the tab is found by clicking "Interact with Contracts" in the Tools section dropdown.

In the selector, choose the Custom option for the contract options and copy the address and ABI of the Claims contract. The next slide will display this information for you. If you want to verify it yourself you can view it on Etherscan verified contracts.

Entering Inputs

Select the claim function from the dropdown and enter the address of the Ethereum account that holds the balance of DOT allocation for which you are going to claim.

The address you are claiming for is usually the same address that you are planning to send the transaction from, in exceptional cases it may be from a different key if you are using an "amended" key.

For the public key field you will use the decoded public key of your address. On the next slide you can input your address into the decoder to get your raw public key.

Sending the Transaction

First unlock your account by selecting the option that matches with the way that you've stored your Ethereum private key. After you've unlocked your account you can continue with the instruction below.

Uncheck the box that says Automatically Calculate Gas Limit and instead use 150000 as the gas limit. You can make changes to the gas price or simply keep it at the default. The network gas price is available at ETH Gas Station.

Click the "Write" button in MyCrypto. In the pop-up confirm the transaction by clicking "Sign Transaction" and send it to the network.

MyCrypto will display a link leading you to a popular block explorer in order to verify your transaction was mined on the network. Once you see it go through you can safely close MyCrypto.

Verifying your Claim

The DOTs are not available to you until Polkadot launches. However, if you would like to verify your claim you can do so below. Enter an Ethereum address, Polkadot address, or Polkadot public key in the input to get the corresponding claim information. Inputting an address will show you the registered Polkadot address, public key, index, and balance of your claim.

If all of this data looks right then your claim was a success! When Polkadot launches, the DOTs will be available automatically in your Polkadot address immediately from genesis.
