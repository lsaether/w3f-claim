    The deadline is XX XXX XXXX. Claims made before this date will allow you to have access to dots as soon as the Polkadot mainnet is launched.

    If you miss the deadline, there is a separate claims process that you can go through (similar to the current Kusama claims process - https://polkadot.js.org/apps/#/claims) which will allow you to claim your dots. You will gain access to your dots very shortly thereafter (most likely within a minute). This process will work indefinitely after the launch of Polkadot’s main network and beyond the genesis block.

  • Why do I need to claim? keyboard_arrow_right

    If you have already purchased dots, you do not yet have access to them. Your “right” to a certain number of dots is currently stored on the Ethereum blockchain as dot allocation indicator tokens. The claims process exists to transform those allocation indicator tokens into dots.

    As part of this claims process you first need to create a Polkadot address, and then prove that you are both the owner of the Ethereum address and the owner of the Polkadot address. After proving that you have access to the dots linked to the Ethereum address, the correct number of dots will be allocated to your address on the Polkadot blockchain.

  • How quickly will I get my dots? keyboard_arrow_right

    If you participate in the claims process before XX XXX XXXX, your dots will be included in the genesis block. This means that you will have access to them as soon as the Polkadot blockchain launches.

    If you claim the dots using the alternative claims process after launch, then you will receive your dots shortly after submitting your claim. This process should take less than a minute.

  • How do I know my claimworked? keyboard_arrow_right

    You should receive a message immediately after attempting to claim your dots, indicating the success or failure of the claim. Once the Polkadot mainnet is running, you can use (https://polkadot.js.org/apps/#/accounts) to verify that your account has the expected number of dots.

  • Can I claim Kusama tokens? keyboard_arrow_right

    If you purchased your dot tokens in the pre-sale, you are entitled to the same number of Kusama tokens as well. You may claim these through a different process separate from this claim page. See (https://guide.kusama.network/en/latest/start/claims/) for detailed instructions.

  • Do I need to claim again if I already claimed for Kusama keyboard_arrow_right

    Yes. Kusama is a completely separate network from Polkadot so any claims for kusamas do not apply to Polkadot or dots. If you already made a claim for kusamas, please make a new claim for dots.

  • Can I claim my dots using through a third party? keyboard_arrow_right

    We do not recommend using a third-party application or process to claim your dots or purchase dots. Please don't hesitate to contact dots@polkadot.network with any questions.

    Claiming using a third-party process can lead to the loss of your allocation, therefore we cannot recommend using any third-party applications to do so. Manually specifying your transaction data, as specified in our claims process, is the only way to be certain you will receive your allocation.
